Introduction

Hugs through Mugs Club

Hugs through Mugs Club is a platform to bring together group of potters to sell their unique and smile inducing pottery. With monthly shows with all it's members who work and create together in the Clay House, these potters have a mission to bring happiness through clay. Based out of San Luis Obispo, but the collective can feauture artists from around the nation.

Target audience

The target audience will inclue the potting communitty of California and across the globe found through social media. Potters of the age range from 16-70 can be found in this targe audience, but most of the users will be from the ages 18-40. There will also be more of a support base in SLO given the location of the studio.

The main goal of Hugs through Mugs Club is to bring attention to local artists and potters around the nation, giving them a space to share and sell their art and build a sense of communitty with like-minded creators. The main task is to bring together a certain collection of artists to put together tri-monthly shows to showcase their work with local communities and to provide a space to sell their work.

Our Mission

Welcome to the Hugs Through Mugs Club. A collaboative group made up of likeminded creators who have come together to help bring some happiness to communites through our diverse pottery. With tri-monthly shows at varrying locations around California and the United States, we share and sell our art hoping to give your daily life a little brighter with or wacky and unique creations.
