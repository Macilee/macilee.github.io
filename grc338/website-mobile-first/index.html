Organic Naturally Leavened Artisan Bread

Come to our storefront, open Wednesday through Saturday 9am - 3pm at 2060A Parker St in San Luis Obispo, California.

At Bread Bike's core is a group of San Luis Obispo community members who subscribe to weekly or bi-weekly deliveries of bread to their homes or local pick-up locations. all bread and pastry is made by hand and delivered by bike the day they are baked.
