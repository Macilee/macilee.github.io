About

Meet Sam

Hi there! This is Sam, one of the bakers and bikers of Bread Bike. I love bread, I love bikes, I love our farmers and this earth, I love the park, I love open bodies of water and I sure as heck love you! Stoked on all things dough and extra stoked on sharing all that dough in all its forms with each and every one of you. Welcome to our Bread Bike!

A Community Supported Bakery

The Bread Bike Community Supported Bakery (CSB) is a San Luis Obispo based bakery ready to provide you with all the sourdough bread of your dreams! We aim to combine a deep love for sharing delicious bread with our community and an equal passion for doing our best to care for the environment. All of our breads are naturally leavened, made with all organic grains, 100% California grown wheat, and shaped/baked with so much love. And to top it off, all products are delivered by bike either directly to your home or to a pick up location nearby.
